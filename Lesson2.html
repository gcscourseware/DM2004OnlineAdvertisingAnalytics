Overview

What is Google Ads?

🔍

Search Ads

Text ads that appear on Google Search results when users search for your target keywords — highest purchase intent.

📷

Display Ads

Visual banner ads shown across millions of partner websites, apps, and YouTube — perfect for brand awareness.

🎬

Video Ads

Engaging video placements on YouTube and across the Google Display Network to capture attention at scale.

💡 Why PPC?

Pay-Per-Click means you only pay when someone actually clicks your ad. Combined with real-time bidding and granular targeting, Google Ads gives businesses of every size measurable, data-driven advertising at scale.

02 Section Two

Creating Effective
PPC Campaigns

Learn keyword strategy, campaign architecture, and the art of writing ads that convert.

Strategy

Keyword Research & Selection

🔍

Match Types

Exact — precise intent.
Phrase — core phrase in order.
Broad — widest reach, broadest targeting.

🚁

Long-Tail Keywords

Longer, specific phrases (e.g. "best running shoes for flat feet") typically have lower CPCs and higher conversion rates.

🔥

Tools to Use

Google Keyword Planner, Ubersuggest, SEMrush, and Ahrefs can uncover search volume, competition, and cost-per-click estimates.

🙺

Negative Keywords

Block irrelevant searches to prevent wasted spend. Example: selling shoes? Add "free" as a negative keyword.

Architecture

Structuring Campaigns

🎨

Campaign

Top level — define budget, targeting, location, and campaign type (Search, Display, Shopping, etc.).

💵

Ad Group

A themed cluster inside a campaign. Each group shares a set of related keywords and a single daily budget.

📝

Ads

The actual creative copy that users see. Every ad group should contain 3–5 ad variations for A/B testing.

💡 Best Practice

Keep ad groups tightly themed — one product or service per group. This improves Quality Scores, lowers CPCs, and makes optimisation far easier down the line.

Copywriting

Writing Effective Ad Copy

📝

Headlines (up to 3)

Lead with your strongest value proposition. Include your primary keyword for relevance. Keep each headline under 30 characters.

💡

Descriptions (up to 2)

Elaborate on benefits, not features. Highlight unique selling points and create urgency. Each up to 90 characters.

✅

Use Ad Extensions

Sitelinks, callouts, and structured snippets give your ad more screen real estate and context.

🔨

Strong CTA

"Shop Now", "Get a Free Quote", "Learn More" — a clear call-to-action guides users to the next step.

📷

A/B Test Always

Run multiple variants simultaneously. Let data decide which headlines and descriptions win over time.

Understand the auction, choose the right bidding strategy, and keep your spend profitable.

Bidding

Types of Bidding Strategies

Strategy How It Works Best For
Target CPA Google sets bids to achieve your desired cost per acquisition. Lead Gen
Target ROAS Bids are optimised to hit a specific return on ad spend. E-commerce
Maximize Clicks Automated bids aim to get the most clicks within budget. Traffic
Manual CPC You set the maximum bid per click — full control. Testing
Enhanced CPC Manual bids are adjusted automatically based on conversion likelihood. Balanced
Finance

Setting & Managing Budgets

💰

Daily Budgets

Set per-campaign daily limits. Google may spend up to 2× on high-performing days, but averages out monthly.

🔩

Budget Allocation

Spread spend across campaigns based on priority. Star performers deserve a larger slice of your total budget.

🎨

Shared Budgets

Pool budget across multiple campaigns so Google can dynamically allocate spend where it performs best.

💡 Adjusting Bids Based on Performance

Monitor CPC, CTR, and conversion rate weekly. Raise bids on high-converting keywords; lower or pause underperformers. Use bid adjustments for time, device, location, and audience segments to fine-tune spend where ROI is highest.

04 Section Four

Tracking & Measuring
Success

Close the loop — set up conversion tracking, read the right metrics, and use analytics to optimise.

Implementation

Setting Up Conversion Tracking

  • 1

    Define Conversion Actions

    Decide what counts — purchases, sign-ups, phone calls, app installs, or page visits.

  • 2

    Install the Conversion Tag

    Add the Google Ads snippet to your website's thank-you or confirmation page via Google Tag Manager.

  • 3

    Set Conversion Values

    Assign a monetary value to each conversion so Google can optimise bids for maximum revenue.

  • 4

    Verify & Test

    Use the Tag Assistant tool to confirm your tags are firing correctly before campaigns go live.

📄

Without conversion tracking, you're flying blind. Accurate data is the foundation for smart bidding, budget decisions, and campaign optimisation.

Deep Dive

Using Google Analytics with Google Ads

📊

Link Accounts

Connect GA4 and Google Ads to unlock cross-platform data. This is a one-time setup under the Admin panel in Analytics.

📍

Audience Insights

See who visits your site, how long they stay, and what pages they browse — then feed that data back into Ads for retargeting.

When Google Analytics and Google Ads work together, you gain full-funnel visibility — from the first ad click through to purchase and beyond. Use GA dashboards to spot drop-off points, refine landing pages, and continuously improve ROI.
