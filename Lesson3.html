Budget Types

Daily vs Lifetime Budgets

ğŸ“…

Daily Budget

The average amount you're willing to spend each day. Facebook paces spend throughout the day to maximise results. Best for ongoing campaigns where you want consistent, predictable daily spend.

ğŸ“Š

Lifetime Budget

A total amount to spend over the campaign's entire duration. Facebook distributes spend across days to optimise delivery. Best for time-limited campaigns like product launches or seasonal sales.
